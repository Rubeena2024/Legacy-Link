Life Lesson 8/9/24

It's june already, I remember last year at this time, I was all into placement preps doing InterviewBit and hackerrank simultaneously, revising Core Cs topics all while trying to complete my summer internship project. I know this time is crucial and hectic but believe me do take breaks and don't let yourself burnout doing same thing(coding/Core Cs) for the whole day keep an alternate schedule if you don't feel like doing a particular task. I want to highlight the importance of revision at this point, keep a note of something new that you came across in a contest question and go through it again before the next contest, that's how you grow. Keep your resume explainably simple - emphasize on major tech stacks and achievements and be ready to answer on any keyword present in it. Projects on your resume should have some purpose, don't keep projects just to showcase a techstack. Remember to keep yourself motivated and learn to pat your own back when no one else does. You are stronger than you think you are !! Keep the below pic in mind. Have faith in your own abilities and keep grinding, You are just two feet from your gold. Hope this placement season things turn out better than the last year

Shriyansh Gaur
